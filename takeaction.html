Helpful Links

Donations

Textile Exchange

Textile Exchange is focused on positively impacting the world by replacing the current unsustainable materials that garments are currently made out of with more sustainable materials to help the environment

Fashion Takes Action

Fashion Takes Action aims to educate its audience on the harmful effects of the fast fashion industry. It does this through holding various programs and events that people can attend to learn more about the cause.

Redress

Redress aims to provide help and support to those who are suffering from these poor working conditions in the factories and workplaces in countries such as Bangladesh and Vietnam

Fair Wear Foundation

The Fair Wear Foundation focuses entirely on improving working conditions for workers in garment factories specifically. They push for changes in government and work with sustainable and ethical brands to acheive their goal.

Educate Yourself

Fashion Revolution

Fashion Revolution is the best resource for a general overview of the issues and problems in the industry. This is the best place to start if you have very limited knowledge on the topic and is suitable for all audiences.

Earth.org

Earth.org focuses specifically on the enviornmental impact of fast fashion and is a great resource if you want a more in depth explanation of these issues

Princeton

This source is also great for learning about the environmental impacts of fast fashion and it goes into a lot of depth about the specifica aspects of fast fashion that harm the enviornment

Good on You

This source is good because it provides a general overview of all issues, especially those that aren't described in other articles, such as issues with animals. It also provides alternative ethical sites to use instead.

Ethical Alternatives

Day 6

This brand focuses on creating clothing that will minimize environmental risk and creates comfortable, pajama style clothing

White Stuff

White Stuff uses mostly recycled materials to make their product making them both ethically manufactured and environmentally sustainable for its customers

Bogdar

Bogdar is a European clothing brand that focuses on the labor methods to make their garments. They own a very small business, so they don't use any of those unethical practices that the larger companies do.

Nobody's Child

Nobody's Child uses entirely sustainable materials to make its clothing and is also relatively affordable in terms of price. This would be a good option for anybody who doesn't have the capacity to spend that much money on clothing.

OMNES

OMNES is focused on creating clothes that will last a very long time. It also prioritizes the Health and Safety standards of each country that it is located in
